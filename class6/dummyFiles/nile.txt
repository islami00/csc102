Nile University of Nigeria (NILE) is a private university located in Abuja, Nigeria. The University was established in 2009. 
At the time of formation, NILE had three faculties (Arts & Social Sciences, Engineering, and Natural & Applied Sciences) and 93 students.
Currently, Nile University of Nigeria has six faculties and a School of Postgraduate Studies, as well as over 4,000 students (undergraduate and postgraduate).
NILE currently offers 33 undergraduate programs and 43 postgraduate programs.
